Here are a few tips on how to train your Dog

  1. Choose your dog's name wisely and be respectful of it
  2. Decide on the "house rules." Before he comes home, decide what he can and can't do
  3. Set up his private den. He needs "a room of his own."
  4. Help him relax when he comes home.
  5. Teach him to come when called.
  6. Reward his good behavior.
  7. Take care of the jump up. Puppies love to jump up in greeting
  8. Teach him on "dog time."Dogs live in the moment
  9. Discourage him from biting or nipping.
  10. End training sessions on a positive note
